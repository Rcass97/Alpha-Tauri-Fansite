Pierre Gasly

Pierre Gasly is a French racing driver who is currently racing for AlphaTauri-Honda. He made his debut for Toro Rosso at the 2017 Malaysian Grand Prix, replacing Daniil Kvyat. He did not compete at the United States Grand Prix, due to being scheduled in the final race of the 2017 Super Formula Championship, although that race was cancelled due to bad weather. He returned at the Mexican Grand Prix.

Yuki Tsunoda

Yuki Tsunoda (角田 裕毅 in Japanese) is a Japanese racing driver, set to make his debut in the FIA Formula One World Championship for AlphaTauri in 2021. A member of the Honda Formula Dream Project as well as the Red Bull Junior Team, Tsunoda graduated to F1 after claiming the Anthoine Hubert Award in the 2020 Formula 2 Championship.
